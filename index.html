Product Concept

ThriftLink

A startup concept developed to support local thrift stores through a centralized online shopping platform. We will be helping stores gain digital literacy skills and analytics showing them what customers click on, what items perform well, and helping them build a strong online presence. So while the product looks like a website, it’s really a service that gives thrift stores the tools and reach they need to connect with shoppers who already want what they sell.
